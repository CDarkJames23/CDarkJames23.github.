PICK YOUR ADVENTURE!

Whether you want your first taste of white water or you want to revisit the basics of paddling on moving water, we are here to meet your needs.

All courses start with an introduction to your equipment using a range of high quality kayaks. After a fitting session we will take you on some easy water and start to coach you in the skills required for white water paddling. From there we will move you on at your own pace setting challenges that will suit you.
